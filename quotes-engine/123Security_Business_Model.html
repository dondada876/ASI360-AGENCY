Core Innovation
The Virtual 360 Assessment Pipeline
Step 1 — Discover

Customer Finds 123Security

Through SEO content (security tips, crime reports, DIY guides), Google Business, or referral. They explore the free security resources, browse the equipment marketplace, check their neighborhood crime map.

Revenue: $0 (lead generation). Content marketing drives organic traffic. Free crime report integration pulls from local PD APIs and builds trust.
Step 2 — Assess

Book a Virtual 360 Assessment

Customer books online. Two options: (A) Self-scan — we ship a 360 camera, they walk the property on a video call with our team, then ship back. (B) iGUIDE scan — we ship the iGUIDE 360 device, customer scans the entire facility getting precise measurements, floor plans, and 3D mapping, then returns the box.

Revenue: Assessment fee ($199-499 depending on property size). Refundable against project purchase. iGUIDE scan provides architectural-grade floor plans that become the digital twin for system design.
Step 3 — Design

ASI 360 Creates the Security Plan

Using the 360 scan data, our team designs a complete security solution: camera placement, access control points, NVR sizing, monitoring zones. The digital scan means we never need to visit the site for the design phase.

The 360 scan is the secret weapon. We now have measurements, sight lines, entry points, and a virtual walkthrough. We can design remotely for ANY location — SF, Vegas, Jamaica, anywhere.
Step 4 — Purchase

Customer Buys the Package

Hardware package ships direct from ADI/distributor to the job site. Luminys cameras, Keri access control, networking, ASI 360 Hub — all pre-configured based on the 360 scan design. Customer sees exact pricing upfront.

Revenue: Hardware sales at 50-56% margin. Package includes everything needed for installation. Pre-configured means faster install, fewer truck rolls, fewer callbacks.
Step 5 — Install

Dispatch Local Contractors

"DoorDash for security labor." We dispatch vetted local electricians and low-voltage contractors. They get the 360 scan digital twin with exact placement coordinates, wiring paths, and install instructions. They do the physical work; we manage the project remotely.

Revenue: 30% margin on labor. Contractor gets 70%, ASI 360 gets 30% for project management, QA, and the digital twin that makes the install straightforward. Contractors love it — we bring them jobs with pre-engineered plans.
Step 6 — Monitor

Activate Managed Monitoring

ASI 360 Hub goes live. RRMS Triangulum AI begins automated monitoring. Customer gets mobile alerts, video verification, and 24/7 human backup. Monthly recurring revenue begins.

Revenue: $50-200/site/month MRR. This is where enterprise value is built. 100 monitored sites at $100/mo = $120K/year recurring. Compounds every month as new installs complete.
